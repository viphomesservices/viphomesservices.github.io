ABOUT THE COMPANY

VIP Home Services is a licensed home cleaning & organizing business. We serve the residential and commerical clients of greater Vancouver and the North Shore. Our main priority is to address the needs of our clients and exceed their expecations. As home owners ourselves, we empathize with the worries and concerns of our clients. Our professional team of cleaners are trained to work ethically, carefully, and efficiently. As a result, we are fully committed to treating you and your home with the same level of care and respect that we expect for ours.

Reliable

Reliability is our commitiment to professionalism. Our clients feel more comfortable knowing what to expect.

Trust

Trust is key for strong relationships. We work just as hard to maintain your trust as we do to earn it.

Care

Care is our main priority at any location. Your home is your property, and we take every stride to treat is as such.

Detail

Attention to detail matters. Recognizing the small things makes a big difference.
